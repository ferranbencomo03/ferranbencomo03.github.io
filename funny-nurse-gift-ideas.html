15 funny nurse gift ideas they'll actually love

Perfect for nurse week, graduation, or a hardworking coworker.

The best nurse gifts are funny, affordable, and — crucially — something they'll actually use on a 12-hour shift. Skip the generic "world's best nurse" plaque. Here are 15 ideas that get a real laugh and a real "thank you."

Desk & shift essentials

  • A funny mouse pad for the charting station — humor they see every shift.
  • A coffee mug with a nurse pun (because caffeine is a food group).
  • A roomy tote bag for clinicals, scrubs and snacks.
  • A good badge reel or retractable ID holder.
  • Compression socks that don't look medical.

Funny & sarcastic picks

  • "Chart now, cry later" anything — it's the unofficial nurse motto.
  • "Nurses call the shots" — a classic that always lands.
  • "But first, coffee… then I save lives."
  • A sarcastic enamel pin for the lanyard.
  • A funny ornament for the holidays.

Thoughtful extras

  • A study guide or cheat sheet bundle for a nursing student or new grad.
  • A nice pen set (nurses lose pens constantly).
  • A water bottle that survives a shift.
  • Hand cream for hands washed 50 times a day.
  • A gift card paired with a small funny item.
