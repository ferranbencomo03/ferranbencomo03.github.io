Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how BrightOwlGoods ("we", "us", "our") handles information in connection with our application (the "App") that uses the Pinterest API to publish Pins of our own products to Pinterest. By connecting a Pinterest account to the App, you agree to this Policy.

1. Who we are

BrightOwlGoods is a small independent shop that creates digital study guides, printables and print-on-demand gifts. The App is an in-house tool we use to schedule and publish Pins to our own Pinterest boards.

2. What data we collect, and for how long

  • Pinterest account data (via OAuth): when a Pinterest account is connected, Pinterest provides us with an access token and basic account information (your account ID and the list of your boards). We use these only to publish Pins on your behalf.
  • Content you provide: the images, titles, descriptions, destination links and board selections for the Pins you choose to create.
  • Operational logs: minimal technical records of whether a Pin was published successfully or returned an error, so the tool can work reliably.
  • We do not collect payment data, browsing history, contacts, or any special categories of personal data.
  • Retention: the access token and Pinterest-derived data (board lists, account identifiers) are kept only while your account stays connected. Operational logs are kept for up to 90 days and then deleted.

3. How we use the data

  • To authenticate with Pinterest (OAuth) and to create and publish Pins to your boards through the Pinterest API.
  • To show you basic success/error status of your Pins (simple analytics about whether posting worked).
  • We do not use Pinterest data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to publishing the Pins you request.

4. What happens to Pinterest data when you disconnect

You can disconnect at any time from within the App or by revoking access in your Pinterest account settings. When you disconnect, we delete the stored access token and any cached Pinterest-derived data (board lists, account identifiers) from our systems within 30 days. Pins already published remain on your Pinterest account until you remove them there.

5. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell, rent, resell, or redistribute Pinterest content or Pinterest-derived data to any third party. We do not share your data with anyone except the infrastructure providers strictly necessary to run the App (for example, secure hosting), or where we are legally required to do so.

6. How to contact us about your data

For any question or request regarding your data — including access or deletion — email us at brightowlgoods@gmail.com. We will respond within a reasonable time.

7. Pinterest disclaimer

This App uses the Pinterest API but is not endorsed by, sponsored by, or affiliated with Pinterest, Inc. "Pinterest" is a trademark of Pinterest, Inc. All Pinterest content accessed through the App belongs to its respective owners.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will reflect any changes, and continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised Policy.
