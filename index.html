About us

BrightOwlGoods is a small independent studio that designs digital study guides and printables (nursing, exam prep, planners) and print-on-demand gifts (mugs, totes, ornaments and more) for nurses, students, teachers and other hard-working people.

We sell our products through our Etsy shop and share them on Pinterest.
